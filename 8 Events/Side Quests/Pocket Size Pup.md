A [[Halfling]] Farmer Was Having Trouble With Their Beloved Dog They Were Just to Big To Handle! Until A Wizard, Wandering By, Offered A Solution. To Shrink The Hound! The Farmer Bought A Scroll And Used It. The Spell Was Too Strong And Now the Poor Pooch Barely Fills A Teacup. The Dog Is As Happy As Ever, But It's Not Safe To Keep Them So Small. Will The Party Keep Their Beloved Dog Safe And Deliver Them To The Wizards Home. They Should be Able To Reverse The Spell.
