A Peaceful Village Is Plagued By Nightly [[Goblin]] Raids Targeting The Market Square. The Goblins Steal Food, Leaving Stalls Overturned And Villagers Divided On How To Respond. The Adventurers Are Hired To Investigate And Stop The Raid. Will They Uncover the Goblins' Plight Or Eliminate Them As A Threat?

### Adventure Hook
Choose One Or More Of The Following Hooks To Draw The Party Into The Story. The Town Will Offer A Bounty Of **100 GP** To Resolve The Matter, Regardless Of Who Is Spoken To:
- **Merchant's Plea.** A Merchant Offers A Free Meal Every Day To Stop The Goblins
- **Militia Request.** The Captain Of The Village Guard Asks The Party For Help. As His Forces Are Overstretched.
- **Personal Appeal.** A Young Child Asks The Adventurers To Recover Their Family's Stolen Food.

### Market [[Investigation]] 
The Group Can Explore The Market Square And Interact With Its Wary Inhabitants To Uncover Clues. About The [[Goblin]] Raids. Success In Any One Of These Areas Provides Partial Information, But The Party Must Interpret The Clues To Follow The Goblins' Trail.
###### Ask Around
They Find Bryn, A Young Street Sweeper. A Successful **[[Persuasion]] Check (DC10)** Reveals He Saw Goblins Carrying Sacks Of Food Towards The Forest. Failure Makes Bryn Anxious, Suggesting He Overheard They Escaped Towards The West Fields Instead.
###### Search The Market
A Sharp Eye (**[[Investigation]] Check DC 10**) Spots Half-Eaten Food Scraps Leading Toward The Forest. Failure Misinterprets The Clues Suggesting The Goblins Fled Toward The West Fields.
###### Follow The Tracks
Small Clawed Footprints Crisscross The Market. A **[[Survival]] Check (DC 12)** Confirms They lead To The Forest, But Failure Confuses The Trail, Pointing To The West Fields Instead.

### Complication: A False Path
The Party Finds Themselves In The Expansive West Fields, Which show No Sign Of [[Goblin]] Activity. Instead, They [[Encounter]] A Group Of Three Farmers, Armed With Improvised Weapons (Commoner Stat Block). Mistaking Then For Raiders, The Party Must Pass A Group Check (**[[Persuasion]] Or [[Intimidation]], DC 12**) To Calm The Farmers. Failure Results In A Scuffle As The Farmers Attack In Panic.
###### [[Goblin]] [[Reaction]]
If The Party Is Delayed, The Goblins Strengthen Their Defenses:
- **Additional Traps.** The Goblins Add An Extra Snare Trap And Sharpen Pit Stakes Near The Camp
- **Increased Guards.** One Extra [[Goblin]] Are Posted As Sentries At The Camp Entrance.

### Forest Trail
Once The Party Finds The Correct Trail, They Enter A Dense Forest Marked By [[Goblin]] Activity. While Making Their Way, T[[Encounter|hey Encou]]nter A **Snare Trap** (Increased To Two If Delayed By The False Path) A **[[Perception]] Check (DC 10)** Is Needed To Spot Each Trap. Failure Results In A Character Being Hoisted 10 Feet Into The Air. If A Character Is Captured. They Have 1 Minute To Escape Before Sentries Ambush The Group.

### [[Goblin]] Camp
The [[Goblin]] Camp Lies In A Shallow Cave Made Of Two Rooms. There Are Emaciated [[Goblin]] Huddles Around A Small Fire, Surrounded By Meager Stolen Food. A Leader Named Gribnok Will Approach. If The Group Took Out The Sentries, He And The Others Will Be On Edge.
###### Options For The Party
- **Diplomacy.** Gribnok Explains They Were Driven From Their Home By A Predator. They Promise To Leave If Provided With A Month's Worth Of Supplies. If The Sentries Were Killed A **[[Persuasion]] Check (DC 12)** Is Needed To Calm Their Fears.
- **Combat.** Gribnok Leads Two Goblins In Defense (All Use **Goblin** Stat blocks). If Gribnok Is Defeated, The Remaining Goblins Will Flee

### Conclusion
Th Adventure Concludes Based On The Party's Actions:
- **Negotiation.** The Goblins Stop Raiding And Reward The Party With A Magical Amulet And A Rune Marked Tooth, Hinting At The Predator That Displaced Them.
- **Elimination.** The Goblins Are Wiped Out, Leaving Behind Meager Loot And The Strange Tooth. An **[[Investigation]] Check (DC 12)** Is Needed To Find The Magical Amulet.
- **Unresolved.** If The Party Does Nothing, The Raids Escalate, And Tensions I The Village Worsen. The Adventurers Do Not Get Paid.
