The stretched skin of this undead warrior is purple and bruised. Clad in broken armor, a deep gash in its breastplate marks the lethal blow that ended its life. Eldritch magic wells within the wound. As it catches sight of you, its face twists into a hateful scowl. Its dead eyes burn with spectral energy and contaminated magic seeps from blackened claws. In a cracked voice, it howls a delusional challenge: "To arms, comrades! Drakkenheim will be free of these monsters!"

Madness awaits those who too often venture into the ruins. Not even the dead are spared, as those who fall here may rise again as Haze Wights. These undead retain a semblance of their former intellect and personality but twisted by delusion, despair, and malice. Imbued with contaminated power, they often raise Haze Husks to fight alongside them.

Many Haze Wights believe they are great heroes rallying the common folk against invaders, but in their madness, they see all humanoids as monsters. Like their Haze Husk minions, Haze Wights generally ignore contaminated monsters and treat them like "fair citizens" of the city. Groups of Haze Wights may act as if they are still a party of explorers, monster hunters, or part of a military expedition. In battle, they launch contaminated energy as if making skillful sword swipes, deft crossbow fire, or streaking magical blasts.

Haze Wights are often encountered in groups including a small retinue of Haze Husks and sometimes even an Arcane Wraith. In their madness, they believe their ability to reanimate Haze Husks is an inspiring battle command, a prayer of healing, or a call for reinforcements.

### Reanimated by the Haze

Left undisturbed in the ruins of Drakkenheim, the corpse of a humanoid character of 6th level (or Challenge Rating 6) or higher has a 10 percent chance each day of rising as a Haze Wight.

### Study the Foe

Characters proficient in Arcana gain [[Advantage]] on Intelligence (Religion) checks to [[Study Action]] a Haze Wight, learning the following based on their result:

(10+) Delusional Undead. You recognize that these creatures think they are still alive, and that we are the monsters!

(15+) Contaminated Eldritch Power. You are aware of the Haze Wight's Contaminated Blast.

(20+) Awaken Minions. You know of the creature's Animate Haze Husk Bonus Action.

(25+) Made With Delerium. You understand that it's possible to create these zombies with delerium and the [[Animate Dead]] spell (see the Creating Haze Husks sidebar).

### Combat Tactics

Haze Wights are intelligent warriors who can engage in melee or ranged combat. However, they mistakenly believe their foes are mindless monsters not requiring careful tactics, seeing most targets as beasts to be put down. When a foe demonstrates unexpected magic or strategy, some fight more carefully, commanding minions to surround and corner their opponents.

Groups of these wights sometimes believe they are still-living, acting as if they still possess their former abilities, using teamwork and "healing" each other by reanimating the fallen as Haze Husks. Nevertheless, their delusions render them incapable of imagining defeat; they do not flee when wounded, nor when allies fall. Instead, they believe they fight in a heroic last stand, desperately rallying against the inevitable doom they already suffered.
