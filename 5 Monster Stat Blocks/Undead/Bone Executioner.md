#### **<u>Features</u>**  
**Undead Fortitude:** If Reduced To 0HP, The Executioner Makes A *Constitution* Saving Throw (DC 5 + Damage Taken). On A Success, It Drops To 1HP Instead, Unless The Damage Was Radiant Or A [[Critical Hit]].

**Bone-Crushing Strike:** Deals An Additional +2 *Bludgeoning* Damage To Targets Wearing [[Heavy Armor]] Or With Natural Armor (Such As Dragons Or Golems)

**Executioner’s Presence:** When The Bone Executioner Scores A [[Critical Hit]], All Creatures Of Its Choice Within 10ft Must Succeed On A DC 13 *Wisdom* Saving Throw Or Be *[[Frightened]]* Until The End Of Their Next Turn.
#### <u>Actions</u> 

**Great Maul:** Melee Weapon Attack: +6 To Hit, Reach 5ft, One Target. Hit: 14 (2d8+4) *Bludgeoning* Damage.

**Skull-Crusher (Recharge 5-6 Turn):** The Executioner Makes A Devastating Overhead Smash. The Target Must Succeed ON A DC 14 *Strength* Saving Throw Or Be Knocked *[[Prone]]* And *[[Stunned]]* Until Start Of It’s Next Turn. Hit: 18 (3d8+4) *Bludgeoning Damage*. 

#### Variant: Execution Surge
Use This Variant When You Want To Create A More Dangerous Elite Version Of The Bone Executioner, Or When You Need To Escalate Tension Mid-Combat After It Downs A Party Member.

**Execution Surge:**
When The Bone Executioner Reduces A Creature To 0HP, It Enters A Brutal Trance Of Undeath Until The End Of Combat: 
- Its Great Maul Deals And Extra +1d8 *Bludgeoning* Damage On Hit
- Its Skull-Crusher Ability Now Recharges On A 4-6 Rounds Instead Of  5-6 Rounds.
