#### **<u>Actions</u> 

**Multiattack.** The Dragon Makes Three Rend Attacks. It Can Replace Two Attacks With A Use Of Sleep Breath.
**Rend.** *Melee [[Attack Rolls]]:* +7, Reach 10ft. *Hit:* 15 (2d10+4) Slashing Damage.
**Fire Breath (Recharge 5 Rounds)** *Dexterity Saving Throw: DC 14,* Each Creature In A 40ft Long, 5ft Wide Line. *Failure* 38 (11d6) Fire Damage. *Success:* Half Damage.
**Sleep Breath.** *Constitution Saving Throw:* DC 14, Each Creature In A 30ft Cone. *Failure:* The Target Is Has The [[Incapacitated]] Condition Until The End Of Its Next Turn, At Which Point It Repeats The Save. *Second Failure:* The target Has The [[Unconscious]] Condition For 1 Minute. This Effect Ends For The Target If It Takes Damage Or A Creature Within 5ft Of IT Takes An Action To Wake It.
