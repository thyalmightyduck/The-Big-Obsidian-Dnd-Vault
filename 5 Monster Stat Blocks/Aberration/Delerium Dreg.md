This wretched figure has been twisted and warped by eldritch contamination. Beneath their shredded and torn clothing, you can see all manner of sores, lesions, and bulging blood vessels upon their purple-gray flesh, and their limbs and joints bend at unnatural angles. Their facial features are unnaturally warped into a permanent expression of confused agony, and an octarine glow fills their sunken eyes. Small aberrant mutations appear across their bodies: crystalline bony protrusions and small tendrils bursting forth through their skin, a yellow eyeball blinking open upon their neck, and a disturbingly elongated tongue. As they catch sight of you, they moan painfully and lunge forward.

The typical Delerium Dreg is a disheveled and piteous creature who wanders the ruins in a quasi-addled state. Most have benign mutations that confer no special traits or unique abilities.

### Study the Foe

Characters proficient in Survival or Nature gain [[Advantage]] on Intelligence (Arcana) checks to [[Study Action]] a Delerium Dreg, learning the following based on their result:

(5+) Humans Transformed. You know these Aberrations are immune to further delerium [[Contamination]] but otherwise have no special [[Resistance]].

(10+) Contaminating Claws. You recognize the dangers of the creature's Claw attack.

(15+) Shambling Corruption. You understand the creature's highest and lowest ability scores and saving throws.

### Combat Tactics

Delerium Dregs are desperate and confused combatants, incapable of any complex tactics beyond lunging towards their foes to attack by the most direct means. If they see a creature who is not contaminated, they will attack on sight. However, they will band together to form small herds, shuffling through ruins, or wandering the streets together. A wounded Delerium Dreg typically responds with fury and rage, attacking whatever damaged it last without regard to its personal wellbeing. In very rare cases, a wounded Dreg may attempt to flee, especially from a much more formidable combatant.
