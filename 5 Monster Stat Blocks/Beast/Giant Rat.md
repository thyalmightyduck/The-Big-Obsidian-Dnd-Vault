# **<u>Traits</u>**  
**Pack Tactics.** The rat has [[Advantage]] on an [[Attack Rolls]] against a creature if at least one of the rat's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally doesn't have the [[Incapacitated]] condition.

# <u>Actions</u> 
**Bite:** _Melee [[Attack Rolls]]:_ +5, reach 5 feet. _Hit:_ 5 (1d4 + 3) Piercing damage.


**Habitat:** Forest, Swamp, Underdark, Urban
**Source:** Monster Manuel 2025
