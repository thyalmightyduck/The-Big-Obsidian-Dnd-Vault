# <u>Actions</u> 

**Multiattack.** The spirit makes a number of attacks equal to half this spell's level (round down).

**Radiant Bow (Avenger Only):** _Ranged [[Attack Rolls]]:_ Bonus equals your spell attack modifier, range 600 ft. _Hit:_ 2d6 + 2 + the spell's level Radiant damage.

**Radiant Mace (Defender Only):** _Melee [[Attack Rolls]]:_ Bonus equals your spell attack modifier, reach 5 ft. _Hit:_ 1d10 + 3 + the spell's level Radiant damage, and the spirit can choose itself or another creature it can see within 10 feet of the target. The chosen creature gains 1d10 [[Temporary Hit Point]].

**Healing Touch (1/Day):** The spirit touches another creature. The target regains [[Hit Point]] equal to 2d8 + the spell's level.
