# **<u>Traits</u>**  
**Lightning Absorption.** Whenever the corpsewrought creature is subjected to lightning damage, it takes no damage and regains a number of hit points equal to the lightning damage dealt.

# <u>Actions</u> 
**Slam.** _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +4 plus PB to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 1d8 + 4 bludgeoning damage.
