#### <u>Actions</u> 

**Multiattack.** The Abjuerer Makes Three Burst Magic Attacks
**Arcane Burst.** *Melee Or Ranged Spell Attack:* +6, Reach 5ft., Or Range 120ft., *Hit:* 20 (3d10) Force Damage.
**Force Blast** Each Creature In A 20ft [[Cube]] Originating From The Abjurer Must Make A DC 16 CON Save. On A Failed Save, A Creature Takes 36(8d8) Force Damage And Is Pushed Back 10ft Away From The Abjurer. On A Successful Save, A Creature Takes Half Damage And Isn't Pushed Back.
**Spellcasting.** The Abjurer Casts One Of The Following Spells, Using INT As The Spellcasting Ability (Spell Save DC 16)
*At Will* [[Dancing Lights]], [[Mage Hand]], Message, [[Prestidigitation]]
*2/Day Each:* [[Dispel Magic]], Lightning Bolt, [[Mage Armor]]
*1/day Each* [[Arcane Lock]], [[Banishment]], Globe Of Invulnerability, Wall Of Force

#### **<u>[[Reaction]]</u>** 
**Arcane Ward (Recharge 4 Rounds).** When The Abjurer Or A Creature It Can See Within 30ft Of It Takes Damage, The Abjurer Magically Creates A Protective Barrier Around Itself Or The Other Creature. The Barrier Reduces The Damage To The Protected Creatures By 26(4d10+4), And Then Vanishes
