 Name: Theo Yusuf
Description: Old, Non-binary, [[Umbral Human]], A pixy cut, Short, Stout build, They have tattoos on their hands
Voice: Medium pitch, Soft-spoken, Slow, Speaks in a monotone
Mannerism: They are very spiritual
PlotHook: Witnessed a cult ritual

cursed by [[Ninda Shwarz]] to be a [[Keeper of the Feather]]

Theo was once a humble farmer with a large family all living under one roof. One day a business woman by the name of [[Ninda Shwarz]] came to their farm offering to buy the land, but seeing as the Yusuf family has had it for many generations they politely denied her offer. They did not know Ninda was a women you don’t say no to. Later that year in the dead of winter the family had all woken up to their farm being burned to the ground. Stuck with nowhere to go or place to call home they made their way to [[Ariorith]] to try and find work. 

Unfortunately everywhere they attempted they were met with a cold shoulder. Nobody would look them in the eyes let alone offer them a job. Until they went to Ninda

[[Ninda Shwarz]] welcomed them with warm arms and told them she had a new mine opening up at the [[Verdant Mountains]] where she would hire the whole family. Excited they took the offer and moved to [[Klont]] and got to work.

After Years of tireless work that paid well, Theo and his family went to leave the mines to go back home but the mines were closed off almost like a collapse had happened. His family one by one disappeared never to see the light of day again. 

One day Theo woke up in what could only be explained as a shit hole of a lab where there were giant test tubes and tables with straps laid out everywhere. An almost ethereal darkness enveloped the caves. He was left to his own thoughts for days inside a tube unable to see. 

A Slight light at the center of the room is turned on but to Theo it was blinding. Once his eyes adjusted he could see only one short woman with bright yellow eyes. [[Ninda Shwarz]] was there smiling, as she had gotten exactly what she wanted. Revenge, Labor, And Test subjects for her experiments.
