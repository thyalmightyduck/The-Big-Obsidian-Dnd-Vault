> ##### Description
This quiver contains 5 magic arrows with different properties. This arrow disappears after being shot or 1 minute after being out of the quiver. Each dawn, the quiver creates 1 arrow of your choice. 

𝘽𝙪𝙧𝙣𝙞𝙣𝙜 𝘼𝙧𝙧𝙤𝙬: This arrow deals a additional 2d6 fire damage and flammable objects ignite if they’re not being worn or carried.

𝙁𝙧𝙚𝙚𝙯𝙞𝙣𝙜 𝘼𝙧𝙧𝙤𝙬: This arrow deals and additional 2d6 cold damage and the target has its speed reduced by half until the start of your next turn.

𝘾𝙤𝙧𝙧𝙤𝙨𝙞𝙫𝙚 𝘼𝙧𝙧𝙤𝙬: This arrow deals an additional 2d6 acid damage and the armor class of the target is weakened by 1 until the end of your next turn.

𝙑𝙚𝙣𝙤𝙢𝙤𝙪𝙨 𝘼𝙧𝙧𝙤𝙬: This arrow deals an additional 2d6 poison damage and the target is poisoned until the start of your next turn if it isn’t resistant or immune to poison.

𝙇𝙞𝙜𝙝𝙩𝙣𝙞𝙣𝙜 𝘼𝙧𝙧𝙤𝙬: This arrow deals and additional 2d6 lightning damage and this damage is also dealt to every creature in a 10 ft radius around the target.
