> ##### Description
> This thin, mystical sword appears to be made of crystallized tears and is adorned with tiny bells that ring with sounds from places that no longer exist.


**Magic Weapon:** You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.

**Phantom Path:** While attuned to this weapon, you can use an action to slip into the space between realities. For the next 10 minutes, you can move through solid objects as if they were difficult terrain. You can use this property once per day. If you end your turn inside a solid object, you are shunted to the nearest unoccupied space and take 1d10 force damage.

**Echoing Steps:** Your movement leaves behind spectral footprints that linger for one round. Any allied creature that follows your path during this time uses only 1/2 of their movement speed while following your steps.
