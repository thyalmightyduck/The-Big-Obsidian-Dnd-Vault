> ##### Description
> You gain a bonus to your attack and damage rolls made with this weapon determined by its rarity bonus (RB). _Rare_ (+1), _Very Rare_ (+2), _Legendary_ (+3_)

**Minor Gigantification:** While being a hand crossbow for all intent and purpose, this weapon slightly enlarges its ammunition when fired. As a result, its damage die is a d10 instead of the usual d6

**Major Gigantification:** When you make an attack with this weapon that uses a non-magical ammunition, you can enlarge the ammunition to the size of a siege engine projectile, acquiring the following properties:
- The range for this attack is 100/400 feet.
- It deals additional piercing damage equal to a number of d10 based on this item’s RB, or twice as much if the target is huge or larger.
- If the target is Large or smaller, it must succeed on a Strength saving throw (DC 13+twice this item’s RB) or be pushed 15 ft. in a straight line. If this movement causes it to [[encounter]] a wall or another structure, it is impaled and restrained. An impaled creature can expend half of its speed to end this condition.
- It deals double damage to objects and structers.

You can use this feature once, and you regain its use when you finish a short or long rest. You can only regain this feature through a short rest a number of times equal to this weapon’s RB.
