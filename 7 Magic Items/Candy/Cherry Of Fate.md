> A Small Ruby-Red Cherry, Its Pit Etched With The Symbol Of Infinity. It's Sweet And Sour At Once, Like Two Fates Tangled Together.

#### Effect
After Eating This Cherry, roll 1d20
	**1-9:** For 1 Minute,, You Are Unlucky - Roll With [[Disadvantage]] On All [[Ability Check]], Attacks, And Saves.
	**10-19:** For 1 Minute, You Are [[Lucky (Feat)]] - You Roll With [[Advantage]] On All [[Ability Check]], Attacks, And Saves.
	**20:** For 1 Minute, You May Reroll Any Die Once Per Turn And Take The Better Result.
