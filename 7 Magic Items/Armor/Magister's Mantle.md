> ##### Description
This suspends an engraved metal plate over the wearer’s chest, which bears an enchantment amplifying arcane power and protection from magical attack. 

**Magister’s Wards:** While wearing this robe, you gain a +1 bonus to your AC. This increases to +3  against spell attacks and attacks made with magical weapons
In addition, you gain a +3 bonus to saving throws you make against spells.

**Overwhelming Arcanum:** When you cast counterspell or dispel magic, they are treated as being cast one level higher than the spell slot you expended to cast them. 
When a creature attempts to counterspell as spell you cast, you can expend another spell slot of any level. If you do, the counterspell treats your spell as being that many levels higher than the level at which you cast it.
