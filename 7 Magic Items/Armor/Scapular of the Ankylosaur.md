> ##### Description
This thick monastic scapular is dense and weighty, like a heavy leather apron.   

While wearing it, you have a base AC of 14 + your Dexterity modifier (to a maximum of +2). You can’t benefit from wearing other armor while using this AC.

 **Earthen Slam:** While wearing the scapular, you can use an action to stomp the ground beneath you, sending out a shockwave that affects all other creatures within 15 feet of you. Each creature must make a DC 15 Strength saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 3d6 bludgeoning damage and 3d6 thunder damage and is knocked prone. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage and isn’t knocked prone. This property of the scapular can’t be used again until the next dawn.  
  
**Shield Battery:** While wearing the scapular, you can use a bonus action to magically enhance its protection. You gain a +5 bonus to AC until the start of your next turn or until you remove the scapular. For the duration, whenever a creature hits you with a melee attack, that creature takes 5 bludgeoning damage. This property of the scapular can’t be used again until the next dawn.
