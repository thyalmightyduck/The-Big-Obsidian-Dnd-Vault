> ##### Description
This well hewn +1 steel gauntlet is fashioned from a magically infused steel that is always slightly warm to the touch. The surface of it always seems to shift and dance with radiant patterns of reds. oranges, and purples. A bronze cast recreation of the sun sits boldly towards the top of the gauntlet and the back of the palm is adorned with a similar motif bearing a swirling yellow jewel inside the emits a warming glow at all times.

**Suns Shielding Rays:** Twice per day while you are wearing this gauntlet and are about to be hit by a melee or ranged weapon attack, you can use your reaction to raise this gauntlet and cause it to emit a radiant shield, granting you a +2 bonus to AC until the start of your next turn. Creatures that hit you with a melee weapon attack while this shield is active take 2d6+2 radiant damage.

**Suns Blinding Bash:** If you have activated the shield using a reaction, at the start of your next turn you can attempt to shield bash a creature within 5 feet of you as a bonus action. The creature must attempt a DC 17 Constitution saving throw taking 4d6+4 Radiant damage and becoming blinded until the start of your next turn on a failed save, or half as much damage and not becoming blinded on a successful one. These abilities reset daily at dawn.
