> ##### Description
A elegant porcelain piece of armor that has shattered multiple times be it in battle or from kids knocking it off a podium while playing. Filling the gaps with gold binding to accentuate the scars of this piece of armor.

**AC:** 15 + Dex modifier (Max 2)

This armor negates the additional damage of any critical hit you receive.

In addition, whenever you are hit by an attack while wearing this armor, you gain a +1 bonus to your AC until the end of your next turn.

**Purity:** You cannot be cursed by spells or abilities.

**What doesn’t kill me:** Each time you fall to 0 HP against your will and don’t die while attuned to this armor, your maximum hit points goes up by 10. A golden scar appears on your body. If you try to obtain this blessing voluntarily or abuse this property by hurting yourself or asking someone to do it, the attunement and all blessings cease immediately, and you can’t attune to the armor again.
