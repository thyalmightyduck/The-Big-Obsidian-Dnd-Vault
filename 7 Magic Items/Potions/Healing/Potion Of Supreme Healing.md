### Abilities
This potion is a magic item. As a [[Bonus Action]], you can drink it or administer it to another creature within 5 feet of yourself. The creature that drinks the magical red fluid in this vial regains 20d4 + 20 [[Hit Point]]. The potion's red liquid glimmers when agitated.
