> ##### Description
The Interior of these boots is lined with an extremely comfortable mix of cotton and leather making them feel especially agreeable to wear. The exterior seems to be composed of a still living wood that grows into leafy sprigs toward the collars of the boot. When you don these shoes, the wood grows and contorts to wrap itself to you making a snug, but sure fit.

While wearing these magic boots, you gain a +5 foot bonus to any walking or climbing speeds that you have. Additionally, you are unaffected by difficult terrain that is caused by natural flora such as plants or fungi.

When you move while wearing these boots, you can cause the roots of it to extend allowing you to move up to your speed in a singular step. This allows you to clear horizontal gaps or hazards so long as they are no wider than your base walking speed. Dashing has no effect on the amount of distance you can cover with these boots.
