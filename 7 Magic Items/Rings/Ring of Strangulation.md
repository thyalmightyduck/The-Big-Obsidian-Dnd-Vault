> ##### Description
When you equip this ring, it creates an exact replica on your opposite finger. Removing any of these two rings makes the other one disappear. If they are removed simultaneously, they both disappear.

**Unseen Danger:** These rings are worn with the largest gems toward the inside of your hand. While worn, they can’t be detected or identified by magical means. A creature can attempt a DC 20 Arcana check to determine they are magical.

**Grip of Death:** While wearing these rings, you gain a +2 bonus to any ability checks made to grapple a creature or keep it grappled, as well as to the escape DC of your grapple.

**Garrote:** As a free action while you have both hands free, you can summon a magical wire between both rings. When you successfully grapple a creature who is surprised, doesn’t see you or isn’t hostile, you can use the wire to garrote them. A creature garroted in this way can’t speak, scream or cast spells that has a vocal component. A grapple creature take 1d10 necrotic damage when you first grapple them, and you can use your action to repeat this damage on a grappled target on subsequent turns. If you are a rogue, you can add the damage of your sneak attack to this damage.
