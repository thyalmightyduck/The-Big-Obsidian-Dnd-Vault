> ##### Description
A silver ring with infinite knowledge has an open book with multiple pages.

**Arcanist’s Library:** As an action you can magically store one book inside an extra-dimensional space tied to this ring. You can store up to 30 books in the extradimensional space. You can use another action to retrieve one of the books from this space.

**Teacher’s Patience:** Whilst wearing this ring you receive a +2 bonus on Concentration checks.

**Magical Mentor:** Whilst wearing this ring you can spend 10 minutes talking to a group of up to 20 creatures. At the end of the 10 minutes roll a Charisma check (DC 10). On a success all creatures that were paying attention gain a bonus to their Intelligence score equal to half your Intelligence modifier (rounded up). This bonus lasts for 12 hours, you can use this feature once per long rest.
