> ##### Description
Like they say, “It’s always best to keep a shield on hand.”

When you attune to this ring, you can hold a shield for the duration of the attunement (you must already be attuned to the shield if it requires attunement). if you do so, the shield becomes bonded to this ring and becomes magically stored within it.

If you bond a different shield to this ring, the previously bonded shield is no longer bonded and appears in an unoccupied space within 5 feet of you.

As a bonus action while wearing this ring, you can have the bonded shield appear in your empty hand or dismiss it (no action required). You cannot be disarmed of the bonded shield.

**Protective Reflex:** So long as you are not surprised or incapacitated, when you are hit by an attack while wearing this ring you can use your reaction to have the bonded shield appear as normal, potentially turning the triggering attack into a miss.
