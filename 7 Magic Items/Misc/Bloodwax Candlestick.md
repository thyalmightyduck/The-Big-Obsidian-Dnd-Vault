
This Candlestick’s Magic is activated when the candle is lit, which requires an action. You can have this candle burn for 1 hour, either all at once, or in several shorter uses, each one using a minimum of 1 minute from the duration. The flame cannot be extinguished by normal means, such as water or wind. When you finish a long rest, you can expend a hit die to have this candle replenish itself.

**Revealing Light:** When lit, the candle sheds bright light in a 30-foot radius and dim light for an additional 10 feet. Creatures and objects within the bright light can’t benefit from being invisible, and you can see creatures within the ethereal plane within that radius.

**Flash Burn:** As an action, you can cause this candle to burn more intensely, warding off the terrors of the dark. This instantly consumes 15 minutes of the remaining time from the candle.

Frightened creatures of your choice within the bright light of this candle, immediately make a saving throw against the [[Frightened]] condition.
