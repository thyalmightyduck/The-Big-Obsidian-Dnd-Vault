> ##### Description
This emerald bracelet is shaped like a small spider wrapping around the wearer’s wrist. It’s tiny legs move as if alive, yet it never leaves its owner

**Web Shot:** As an action, the bracelet can shoot a line of strong silk up to 30 feet long. It can be used to swing, climb, or grab objects, functioning as a sturdy rope. The web dissolves after 1 minute.

**Sticky Web (2/Day):** As an action, you can cast a web that covers a 10-foot area within 30 feet. Any creature entering the area must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or become restrained until the end of its next turn.

**Spider’s Instinct:** While wearing the bracelet, you gain advantage on Perception checks to detects traps or hidden creatures.
