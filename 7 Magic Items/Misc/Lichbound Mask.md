> ##### Description
> This ominous mask is crafted from pale, corpse-like bone, reinforced with dark arcane metal. Dried, vein-like, runes run across its surface, glowing with a cold violet light whenever undead are near. Wisps of necrotic energy drift from it’s eye sockets, and anyone who looks  into them briefly glimpses the shifting echoes of forgotten souls.

**Touch Of The Grave:** Once per long rest, when you hit a creature with a spell attack, you can deal an additional 2d6 necrotic damage.

**Whispers of the Lich:** During a short rest, faint undead murmurs grant you fragments of forbidden knowledge, you can replace one prepared spell with another from your spell list. (one you could normally prepare).
