This Gilded Tent unfurls to a wondrous interior, sure to make any adventurer safe and comfortable after a long journey.

**Glamping:** When you toss this tent into a flat floor, it instantly pitches itself and stands upright. The interior of this tent is akin to a Magnificent Mansion under your control with the following benefits:
- You have advantage on saving throws while inside the tent.
- Other creatures in the tent have disadvantage on all saving throws against spells you cast and abilities you use.
- Creatures inside the tent cannot be targeted by any divination magic or perceived through magical scrying sensors other than your own.

This property ends after 24 hours or if you mentally will it. When this property ends, the tent folds itself up and the golden sigils fade away. The sigils return over the course of 7 days, afterwhich the tent may be used again.
