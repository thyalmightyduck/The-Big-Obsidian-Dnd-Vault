> ##### Description
“Watch the stars, and see yourself running with them.”

This golden collar is fashioned into the shape of a slatted necklace of delicate design. One of its four layers is a smooth cold while rock the bears some hallmarks of an arcane origin. While examining the inner collar while under shadow or darkness, vast nebulas and distant stars are revealed to the naked eye.

- this must be worn to function.
- To activate this boon, the user must entirely deplete their spell slots and then take a long rest while gripping the collar in an attempt to unlock its depth of arcane secrets.

**Spell-Slinger:** This item grants an additional +1 to all of the spell save DC’s originating from the user.

**Gleamed Secrets:** While wearing this, the user is capable of performing ritual spells in half the additional time normally needed (5 mins).
