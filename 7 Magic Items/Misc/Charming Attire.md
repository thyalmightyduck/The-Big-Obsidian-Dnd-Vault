> ##### Description
> Embroidered attire, evoking nobility, worn for matrimonial ceremonies. This item has 6 charges and regains all expended charges when you finish a long rest.

**Noble Presence:** You have advantage on Charisma ([[Performance]] and [[Persuasion]]) check you make while wearing this clothing. While under the effects of the Ceremony spell, you add you Proficiency Bonus to such checks.

**Captivating Look:** When a creature within 30 feet of you that can see you makes a saving throw to avoid or end the charmed condition, you can spend a charge as a reaction to cause the creature to roll their saving throw with disadvantage. While you are under the effects of the Ceremony spell, the creature also reduces the number by 1d4.

_Wedding attire has come in many forms throughout the ages. Pieces may be inherited by culture or chosen by the wearer to fit the vision of their perfect day. While a fine suit or beautiful gown on its own can be enough to dazzle the crowd, a little magic never hurts._
