> ##### Description
The gold lining of this dealer’s card shoe is inlaid with ornate pattern work that is meticulously well polished to a bright shine. A small and clever device on the side of the shoe function to push a card up quickly when the top of the shoe is opened allowing for an easy draw at a moments notice. The back of this shoe is equipped wit a sturdy leather belt loop that can be fastened on your waist for convenient access whenever a card needs to be drawn. Inside the shoe is what looks to be a deck of playing cards.

This shoe contains a standard deck of playing cards that can be used for any card game. The shoe itself is magically enchanted to reshuffle the deck within it each time a card is drawn. Three times per day, as a bonus action, you can speak the command word and draw a card from the shoe giving you additional effects depending on the suite of the card drawn. Roll a d4 to determine the suite and effect.

- **Hearts:** You heal 8d4+8 hit points.
- **Diamonds:** You gain a +1 bonus to your AC until the end of your next turn.
- **Clubs:** You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls until the end of your next turn.
- **Spades:** You can immediately move up to your movement speed without triggering attacks of opportunity.
