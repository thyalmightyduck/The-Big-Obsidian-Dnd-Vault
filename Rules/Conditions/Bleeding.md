**Ongoing Damage.** At the end of each of your turns, you lose [[Hit Point]]. The number of [[Hit Point]] lost is indicated by the condition, such as "Bleeding 2d4."

**Regaining Hit Points.** The condition ends if you regain any [[Hit Point]].

**Medical Treatment.** You or another creature within 5 feet of you can spend an action and use a healer's kit to staunch the bleeding (no check required), ending the condition.
