### Traits: BoET
- Ability Scores: Dexterity +2; Charisma +1
    
- Creature Type: Humanoid
    
- Size: Medium
    
- Speed: 30 feet
    

Age. Ravenfolk reach adulthood at 10 years old, and can live to be 110.

Alignment. Ravenfolk tend toward chaos thanks to their capriciousness and insatiable curiosity. Greed overwhelms some ravenfolk, drawing them toward evil.

Constant Tune. You have advantage on saving throws against being [charmed](https://5e.tools/conditionsdiseases.html#charmed_phb) or [frightened](https://5e.tools/conditionsdiseases.html#frightened_phb).

Sublime Chord. As an action, you can mimic the mysterious sublime song to charm creatures within range. All creatures within 30 feet that can hear you must succeed on a DC 13 Charisma saving throw or become [charmed](https://5e.tools/conditionsdiseases.html#charmed_phb) by you for 10 minutes. The [charmed](https://5e.tools/conditionsdiseases.html#charmed_phb) creatures regard you as a casual friend. A creature makes this saving throw with advantage if you or your companions are fighting it. Once you use this ability, you cannot do so again until you complete a long rest.

Cosmic Musician. You have proficiency in the Performance skill and gain proficiency with one [musical instrument](https://5e.tools/items.html#musical%20instrument_phb) of your choice.

Languages. You can speak, read, and write Common, Ravenfolk, and Umbral.

Alignment. Sublime ravenfolk more often tend toward lawful alignments than their cousins in the mortal world. They tend to seek stability and connectedness in their relationships, entrusting their path to the song.
