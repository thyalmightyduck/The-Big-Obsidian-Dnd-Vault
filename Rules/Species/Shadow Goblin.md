### Traits: BoET
- Ability Scores: Dexterity +2; Charisma +1
    
- Creature Type: Humanoid
    
- Size: Small
    
- Speed: 30 feet
    

Shadow Goblin Traits. You are a shadow goblin, forever changed by the Shadow Realm, setting you apart from other goblins.

Age. Shadow goblins reach adulthood at age 8 and can live up to 70 years.

Alignment. Much like the shadow fey they coexist with, shadow goblins are not inherently evil, despite their reputations. Their natures are a constant balance between whimsy and being useful and productive. Shadow goblins are more likely to tend toward neutral good or neutral alignments.

Darkvision. You can see in [dim light](https://5e.tools/quickreference.html#bookref-quick,2,vision%20and%20light) within 60 feet as though it were [bright light](https://5e.tools/quickreference.html#bookref-quick,2,vision%20and%20light) and in darkness as if it were [dim light](https://5e.tools/quickreference.html#bookref-quick,2,vision%20and%20light). You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.

Quick Wit. You have proficiency in the Deception and Insight skills.

Shadow Camoflauge. You have advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to hide in [dim light](https://5e.tools/quickreference.html#bookref-quick,2,vision%20and%20light) or darkness.

Stink Eye. As an action, you can perform a combination of rude gestures and sounds to distract one creature you can see within 30 feet. If the target can hear and see you, it must make a Charisma saving throw (DC 8 + your Charisma ability modifier + your proficiency bonus). On a failure, it has disadvantage on the next [[ability check]], [[Attack Rolls]], or saving throw it makes before the start of your next turn.

Sunlight Sensitivity. You have disadvantage on [[attack rolls]] and on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight when you, the target of your attack, or whatever you are trying to perceive is in direct sunlight.

Unseelie Blessing. You have advantage on saving throws against being [charmed](https://5e.tools/conditionsdiseases.html#charmed_phb), and magic can't put you to sleep.

Language. You can speak, read, and write Goblin and Umbral.
