### Traits VSS:PP
- Creature Type: Construct
    
- Size: Small (about 2-3 feet tall) or Medium (about 6 feet tall; Marionette construction only)
    
- Speed: 30 feet
    

As a Geppettin, you have these special traits.

Darkvision. You have Darkvision with a range of 60 feet.

Construct Nature. You don't need food, drink, or air. You don't need to sleep, and magic can't put you to sleep. You can finish a [Long Rest](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#long%20rest_xphb) in 4 hours if you spend those hours in a motionless state, during which you retain consciousness.

Handcrafted Quality. Your design might be unnerving, articulated, or friendly. You have proficiency in the Intimidation, Performance, or Persuasion skill.

Gepettin Construction. For simplicity, geppettin are classified by their materials: bisque are porcelain, marionettes are wood, and plushies are made of fabric. Choose one of the following constructions.

Bisque. You are indistinguishable from a porcelain doll until the moment you strike. Whenever you deal damage to a creature with a weapon [[Attack Rolls]] on your first turn in combat, the creature takes extra damage equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#proficiency_xphb). The damage is the same type dealt by the weapon.

Marionette. Loose strings hang from your jointed limbs. During your turn, your reach is 5 feet greater with any Melee weapon that lacks the Reach, Two-Handed, and Versatile properties.

Plushie. You are stuffed with fluff. When you take Bludgeoning damage, you can take a [Reaction](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#reaction_xphb) to gain [Resistance](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#resistance_xphb) to the triggering damage. You are also knocked 5 feet away from the source of the damage. You can't take this [Reaction](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#reaction_xphb) if you can't be knocked away from the source of the damage.
