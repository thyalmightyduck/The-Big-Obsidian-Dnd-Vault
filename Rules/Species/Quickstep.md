### Traits: BoET
- Ability Scores: Dexterity +2; Charisma +1
    
- Creature Type: Humanoid
    
- Size: Small
    
- Speed: 50 feet

Quickstep Traits. Your quickstep heritage is apparent in a variety of traits you share with other quicksteps.

Age. Quicksteps have remarkably short lifespans compared to other fey. Quicksteps mature around 10 years and live up to 50 years.

Alignment. Due to their association with the shadow fey and their legendary love of competitions, quicksteps have dark reputations, but they are rarely of evil alignment. Many quicksteps have a mischievous, even impetuous, streak that pushes them toward chaotic or neutral alignments.

Darkvision. You can see in [dim light](https://5e.tools/quickreference.html#bookref-quick,2,vision%20and%20light) within 60 feet as though it were [bright light](https://5e.tools/quickreference.html#bookref-quick,2,vision%20and%20light) and in darkness as if it were [dim light](https://5e.tools/quickreference.html#bookref-quick,2,vision%20and%20light). You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.

Fey Ancestry. You have advantage on saving throws against being [charmed](https://5e.tools/conditionsdiseases.html#charmed_phb), and magic can't put you to sleep.

Nimble. You have proficiency in the Acrobatics and Sleight of Hand skills.

Startling Speed. Your movements are so swift that you are almost [invisible](https://5e.tools/conditionsdiseases.html#invisible_phb) when in motion. If you move at least 10 feet on your turn, [[attack rolls]] against you have disadvantage until the start of your next turn. This feature does not function if you are [incapacitated](https://5e.tools/conditionsdiseases.html#incapacitated_phb) or [restrained](https://5e.tools/conditionsdiseases.html#restrained_phb). You can use this ability a number of rounds per day equal to your level.

Languages. You can speak, read, and write Elvish and Umbral.
