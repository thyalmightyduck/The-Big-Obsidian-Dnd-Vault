### Traits: TCM’14
- Ability Scores: Choose one of: (a) Choose any +2; choose any other +1 (b) Choose three different +1
    
- Creature Type: Undead
    
- Size: Medium
    
- Speed: 30 feet
    

Creature Type. You are an Undead.

Dance of Death. As a bonus action, you can make a DC 15 Charisma ([musical instrument](https://5e.tools/items.html#musical%20instrument_phb) or Performance) check. If you succeed, the next [[Attack Rolls]] you make before the end of your turn has advantage. If you succeed on the check by 5 or more, you can roll one of your Hit Dice (without expending it) and gain [[Temporary Hit Point]] equal to the number rolled.

Eternal Party. You don't need to sleep, and you can finish a long rest in 4 hours if you spend those hours enjoying revelry, such as telling stories or playing music.

Moment of Remembrance. When another creature you can see within 30 feet of you fails an [[ability check]], [[Attack Rolls]], or saving throw, you can use your reaction to let the target add 1d4 to the total, potentially causing it to succeed. Once this trait changes a failed roll into a success, you can't use it again until you finish a short or long rest.

Soul of Revelry. You gain proficiency in the Performance skill or with one kind of [musical instrument](https://5e.tools/items.html#musical%20instrument_phb).

Languages. You can speak, read, and write Common and one other language that you and your DM agree is appropriate for your character.

### Traits: TCM’24
- Creature Type: Undead
    
- Size: Medium (about 4-6 feet tall)
    
- Speed: 30 feet
    

Dance of Death. As a [Bonus Action](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#bonus%20action_xphb), you can make a DC 15 Charisma ([Musical Instrument](https://5e.tools/items.html#musical%20instrument_xphb) or Performance) check. If you succeed, the next [[Attack Rolls]] you make before the end of your turn has [Advantage](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#advantage_xphb). If you succeed on the check by 5 or more, you can roll one of your [Hit Point Dice](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#hit%20point%20dice_xphb) (without expending it) and gain [[[Temporary Hit Points]]](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#temporary%20hit%20points_xphb) equal to the number rolled.

Eternal Party. You don't need to sleep, and you can finish a [Long Rest](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#long%20rest_xphb) in 4 hours if you spend those hours enjoying revelry, such as telling stories or playing music.

Moment of Remembrance. When another creature you can see within 30 feet of you fails a [[[D20 Test]]](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#d20%20test_xphb), you can take a [Reaction](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#reaction_xphb) to let the target add 1d4 to the total, potentially causing it to succeed. Once this trait changes a failed roll into a success, you can't use it again until you finish a [Short](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#short%20rest_xphb) or [Long Rest](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#long%20rest_xphb).

Soul of Revelry. You gain proficiency in the Performance skill or with one kind of [Musical Instrument](https://5e.tools/items.html#musical%20instrument_xphb).
