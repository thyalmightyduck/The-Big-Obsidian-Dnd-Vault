### Traits OSW”HAP
- Creature Type: Humanoid
    
- Size: Small (about 2–4 ft tall) or Medium (about 4–7 ft tall), chosen when you select this species
    
- Speed: 30 feet
    

Darkvision. You have Darkvision with a range of 60 ft.

Outdoor Adept. You can move across [Difficult Terrain](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#difficult%20terrain_xphb) made from earth or foliage without expending extra movement.

Protected by Nature. You can attempt to [Hide](https://5e.tools/actions.html#hide_xphb) even when you are only lightly obscured by foliage, heavy rain/snow, mist, or other natural phenomena.

Friend of Fauna. Once per [Long Rest](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#long%20rest_xphb), you can cast the [Beast Bond](https://5e.tools/spells.html#beast%20bond_xge) spell without the material components needed and without expending a spell slot.
