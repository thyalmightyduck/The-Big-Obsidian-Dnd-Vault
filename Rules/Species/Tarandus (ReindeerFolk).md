### Traits: OSW:HAP
- Creature Type: Humanoid
    
- Size: Medium (about 7–8 ft tall, including antlers)
    
- Speed: 35 feet
    

Guiding Light. As a [Bonus Action](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#bonus%20action_xphb), you can produce dim light in a 20 ft radius from your nose. You can end this effect as a [Bonus Action](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#bonus%20action_xphb).

Antler Defence. Once per [Short Rest](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#short%20rest_xphb) as a [Reaction](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#reaction_xphb), you can lower your antlers and enter a defensive stance. Until the start of your next turn, you have a +2 bonus to your [Armor Class](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#armor%20class_xphb) against melee attacks.

Hooves. If you hit a target with an [Unarmed Strike](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#unarmed%20strike_xphb), you can deal bludgeoning damage equal to 1d4 plus your Strength modifier.

Born to Navigate. You have proficiency in the Perception or Survival skill.
