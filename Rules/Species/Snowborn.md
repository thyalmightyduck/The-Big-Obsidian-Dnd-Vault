### Traits: OSW:HAP
- Creature Type: Construct
    
- Size: Small (about 2–4 ft tall) or Medium (about 4–7 ft tall), chosen when you select this species
    
- Speed: 30 feet
    

Chilled Anatomy. You have [Resistance](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#resistance_xphb) to Cold damage. You also know the [Ray of Frost](https://5e.tools/spells.html#ray%20of%20frost_xphb) cantrip.

Compacted Snow. You can move through the space of any creature that is a size larger than you, but you can't stop in the same space. Additionally, you can squeeze through a space that is large enough for a creature one size smaller than you without movement penalties.

Frigid Construct. You don't need to sleep, and magic can't put you to sleep. You can finish a [Long Rest](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#long%20rest_xphb) in 4 hours if you spend those hours unmoving; during this time you are indistinguishable from a snowman, but retain consciousness. You don't need to eat, drink or breathe.

Glacial Form. Once per long rest, you can cast the [False Life](https://5e.tools/spells.html#false%20life_xphb) spell without using the material components or expending a spell slot, and harden your body into ice.
