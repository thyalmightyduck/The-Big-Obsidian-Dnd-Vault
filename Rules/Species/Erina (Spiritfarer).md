### Traits BoET
- Ability Scores: Dexterity +2; Wisdom +2; Choose Dexterity or Charisma +1
    
- Creature Type: Humanoid
    
- Size: Small
    
- Speed: 25 feet
    

Age. Erina reach maturity around 15 years and can live up to 60 years, though some erina burrow elders have memories of events which suggest erina can live much longer.

Alignment. Erina are good-hearted and extremely social creatures who have a difficult time adapting to the laws of other species.

Darkvision. Accustomed to life in the dark burrows of your people, you have superior vision in dark and dim conditions. You can see in [dim light](https://5e.tools/quickreference.html#bookref-quick,2,vision%20and%20light) within 60 feet of you as if it were [bright light](https://5e.tools/quickreference.html#bookref-quick,2,vision%20and%20light), and in darkness as if it were [dim light](https://5e.tools/quickreference.html#bookref-quick,2,vision%20and%20light). You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.

Hardy. The erina diet of snakes and venomous insects has made them hardy. You have advantage on saving throws against poison, and you have resistance against poison damage.

Quills. Your spines are longer and more potent than other erinas. Whenever you make a successful melee attack against a target within 5 feet of you, that target takes an additional 3 (1d6) piercing damage.

Sharp Instincts. You can't be [surprised](https://5e.tools/conditionsdiseases.html#surprised_phb), except when [incapacitated](https://5e.tools/conditionsdiseases.html#incapacitated_phb) by something other than nonmagical sleep

Digger. You have a burrowing speed of 20 feet, but you can use it to move through only earth and sand, not mud, ice, or rock.

Languages. You can speak Erina and Umbral and either Common, Elvish, or Sylvan.

Spirit Tongue. You have an innate knack for speaking to the spirit world. You know the message cantrip. When you reach 3rd level, you can cast the [charm person](https://5e.tools/spells.html#charm%20person_phb) spell with this trait (targeting only undead creatures), and starting at 5th level, you can cast the [enthrall](https://5e.tools/spells.html#enthrall_phb) spell with it. Once you cast a non‑cantrip spell with this trait, you can't do so again until you finish a long rest. Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for these spells.
