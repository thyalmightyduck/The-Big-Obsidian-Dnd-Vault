### Traits: TCM’14
- Ability Scores: Choose one of: (a) Choose any +2; choose any other +1 (b) Choose three different +1
    
- Creature Type: Humanoid
    
- Size: Medium
    
- Speed: 30 feet, Climb equal to your walking speed
    

Speed. Your walking speed is 30 feet, and you have a climbing speed equal to your walking speed.

Bejeweled Carapace. When you are hit with an [[Attack Rolls]], you can use your reaction to add your proficiency bonus to your AC against that attack, potentially causing it to miss. Once you use this trait, you can't do so again until you finish a short or long rest.

Darkvision. You have Darkvision with a range of 60 feet.

Silken Legacy. You know the [[Thaumaturgy]] cantrip. Starting at 3rd level, you can cast the [[Find Familiar]] spell (the familiar takes the form of a jeweled insect or arachnid) using this trait, and starting at 5th level, you can cast the [[Web]] spell using this trait, both without material components. Once you cast [[Find Familiar]] or Web with this trait, you can't cast that spell with it again until you finish a long rest. You can also cast either spell using any spell slots you have of the appropriate level.

Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for the spells you cast with this trait (choose the ability when you select this species).

Spider Climb. You can climb difficult surfaces, including along ceilings, without needing an [[ability check]] and while leaving your hands free.

Languages. You can speak, read, and write Common and one other language that you and your DM agree is appropriate for your character.

### Traits: TCM’24
- Creature Type: Humanoid
    
- Size: Medium (about 5-6 feet tall)
    
- Speed: 30 feet, [[Climb Speed]] equal to your [[Speed]]
    

Bejeweled Carapace. When you are hit with an [[Attack Rolls]], you can take a [[Reaction]] to add your [[Proficiency Bonus]] to your AC against that attack, potentially causing it to miss. Once you use this trait, you can't do so again until you finish a [[Short Rest]] or [[Long Rest]].

Darkvision. You have Darkvision with a range of 60 feet.

Silken Legacy. You know the [[Thaumaturgy]] cantrip. Starting at character level 3, you always have the [[Find Familiar]] spell (the familiar takes the form of a jeweled insect or arachnid) prepared, and starting at character level 5, you always have the [[Web]] spell prepared. You can cast each spell once without a spell slot or Material components. You regain the ability to cast them in that way when you finish a [[Long Rest]]. You can also cast either spell using any spell slots you have of the appropriate level.

Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for the spells you cast with this trait (choose the ability when you select this species).

Spider Climb. You can climb difficult surfaces, including along ceilings, without needing an [[ability check]] and while leaving your hands free.
