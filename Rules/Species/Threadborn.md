### Traits: TCM’14
- Ability Scores: Choose one of: (a) Choose any +2; choose any other +1 (b) Choose three different +1
    
- Creature Type: Construct
    
- Size: Small or Medium
    
- Speed: 30 feet
    

Creature Type. You are a Construct.

Size. You are Medium or Small. You choose the size when you select this species.

Ball-Jointed. You can move through a space as narrow as 1 foot wide as if it were [difficult terrain](https://5e.tools/quickreference.html#bookref-quick,3,difficult%20terrain).

Innocent Mind. You have resistance to psychic damage.

Sewn Nature. You don't require air, food, or drink.

Soothing Heart. You know the [Guidance](https://5e.tools/spells.html#guidance_phb) cantrip. Starting at 3rd level, you can cast the [Sanctuary](https://5e.tools/spells.html#sanctuary_phb) spell using this trait, and starting at 5th level, you can cast the [Calm Emotions](https://5e.tools/spells.html#calm%20emotions_phb) spell using this trait without a material component. Once you cast [Sanctuary](https://5e.tools/spells.html#sanctuary_phb) or Calm Emotions with this trait, you can't cast that spell with it again until you finish a long rest. When you cast [Calm Emotions](https://5e.tools/spells.html#calm%20emotions_phb) in this way, it can target creatures of any type. You can also cast either spell using any spell slots you have of the appropriate level.

Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for the spells you cast with this trait (choose the ability when you select this species).

You've Got a Friend. When a creature you can see within 30 feet of yourself fails an [[ability check]] using a skill, you can use your reaction to cast [Guidance](https://5e.tools/spells.html#guidance_phb) on the creature, ignoring the spell's normal range. The creature can add the spell's 1d4 to its [[ability check]], potentially causing it to succeed. You can use this trait a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.

Languages. You can speak, read, and write Common and one other language that you and your DM agree is appropriate for your character.
### Traits: TCM’24
- Creature Type: Construct
    
- Size: Medium (about 4-7 feet tall) or Small (about 2-4 tall), chosen when you select this species
    
- Speed: 30 feet
    

Ball-Jointed. You can move through a space as narrow as 1 foot wide as if it were [Difficult Terrain](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#difficult%20terrain_xphb).

Innocent Mind. You have [Resistance](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#resistance_xphb) to Psychic damage.

Sewn Nature. You don't require air, food, or drink.

Soothing Heart. You know the [Guidance](https://5e.tools/spells.html#guidance_xphb) cantrip. Starting at character level 3, you always have the [Sanctuary](https://5e.tools/spells.html#sanctuary_xphb) spell prepared, and starting at character level 5, you always have the [Calm Emotions](https://5e.tools/spells.html#calm%20emotions_xphb) spell prepared. You can cast each spell once without a spell slot. When you cast [Calm Emotions](https://5e.tools/spells.html#calm%20emotions_xphb) in this way, it can target creatures of any type. You regain the ability to cast them using this trait when you finish a [Long Rest](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#long%20rest_xphb). You can also cast either spell using any spell slots you have of the appropriate level. Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for the spells you cast with this trait (choose the ability when you select this species).

You've Got a Friend. When a creature you can see within 30 feet of yourself fails an [[ability check]] using a skill, you can take a [Reaction](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#reaction_xphb) to cast [Guidance](https://5e.tools/spells.html#guidance_xphb) on the creature, ignoring the spell's normal range. The creature can add the spell's 1d4 to its [[ability check]], potentially causing it to succeed, then the spell ends. You can use this trait a number of times equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#proficiency_xphb), and you regain all expended uses when you finish a [Long Rest](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#long%20rest_xphb).
