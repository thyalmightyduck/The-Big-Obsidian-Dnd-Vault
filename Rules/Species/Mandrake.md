- Creature Type: Plant
    
- Size: Medium (about 5-7 feet tall)
    
- Speed: 30 feet

As a Mandrake, you have these special traits.

Plant Nature. As long as you are in direct sunlight for at least 4 hours a day, you don't need to eat. Additionally, you can breathe through your leaves and you can absorb water and nutrients through your feet.

Natural Connection. You have proficiency in the Nature or Survival skill.

Root Magic. You know the [[Shillelagh]] cantrip and can target yourself with the spell, treating one of your limbs as a [Club](https://5e.tools/items.html#club_xphb) for the duration of the spell.

When you reach character level 3, you always have [Goodberry](https://5e.tools/spells.html#goodberry_phb) prepared, and when you reach character level 5, you always have [Barkskin](https://5e.tools/spells.html#barkskin_phb) prepared. You can cast each of these spells without a spell slot. Once you cast either spell in this way, you can't cast that spell in this way again until you finish a [Long Rest](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#long%20rest_xphb). You can also cast these spells using spell slots you have of the appropriate level. Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for the spells you cast with this trait (choose the ability when you select this species).

Entangling Vines. You can take a [Bonus Action](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#bonus%20action_xphb) to cause grasping weeds and vines to sprout up from the ground around a Large or smaller creature you can see within 30 feet of yourself. Until the end of the creature's next turn, its [Speed](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#speed_xphb) is 0 and can't increase. This effect ends early if the creature replaces one of its attacks with with freeing itself. You can use this [Bonus Action](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#bonus%20action_xphb) a number of times equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#proficiency_xphb) and you regain all expended uses when you finish a [Long Rest](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#long%20rest_xphb).

When you reach character level 3, your Entangling Vines gain an additional effect based on the season in which you were harvested (choose when you select this species):

Spring. Your Entangling Vines can target an airborne creature within 30 feet of the ground, which is pulled safely to the ground when you use this trait.

Summer. Your Entangling Vines can move the target up to 10 feet to an unoccupied space on the ground or floor.

Autumn. Your Entangling Vines can affect a second creature within 5 feet of the first target.

Winter. The target takes Cold damage equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#proficiency_xphb).
