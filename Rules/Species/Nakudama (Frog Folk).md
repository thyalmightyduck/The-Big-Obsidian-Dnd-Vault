### Traits: 
**Size:** Small (2-3ft)

  

**Speed:** 25 ft and you have a swimming speed of 30 ft.

  
As a Nakudama, you have these special traits.  
  

 **Amphibious**

You can breathe air and water.

**Standing Leap**

Your long jump is up to 20 feet and your high jump is up to 15 feet, with or without a running start.

**Grasping Tongue**

As a bonus action, you can lash out your tongue at an object you can see within 15 feet of you that weighs no more than 5 pounds and pull it to you, catching it in your open hand or allowing it to fall at your feet. If the object is being worn or carried by a creature, the creature must succeed on a contested Strength check or have the object pulled away from it.

**Latching Tongue**

As a bonus action, you can extend your tongue to latch onto a surface or creature that is at least one size larger than you, pulling yourself toward it. The target must be no more than 15 feet away from you.
