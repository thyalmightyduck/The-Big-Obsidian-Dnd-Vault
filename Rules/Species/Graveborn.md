### Traits TCM 2014
- Ability Scores: Choose one of: (a) Choose any +2; choose any other +1 (b) Choose three different +1
    
- Creature Type: Undead
    
- Size: Medium
    
- Speed: 30 feet
    

Creature Type. You are Undead.

Darkvision. You have darkvision with a range of 60 feet.

Devour Corpse. You can eat spoiled and rotten food as if it were fresh.

Additionally, if you spend 1 minute eating flesh from the corpse of a Small or larger creature that isn't a Construct, roll one of your Hit Dice without expending it (even if you have none remaining). You regain a number of hit points equal to the number rolled plus your Constitution modifier (minimum of 1 hit point). Once you regain hit points using this trait, you can't do so again until you finish a short or long rest.

Frozen Waste. You have resistance to cold damage.

Infused Drakkonite. When you damage a creature, you can ignore any resistances it has, and it takes extra necrotic damage equal to your proficiency bonus. Once you use this trait, you can't do so again until you finish a short or long rest.

Languages. You can speak, read, and write Common and one other language that you and your DM agree is appropriate for your character.

### Traits TCM’24
- Creature Type: Undead
    
- Size: Medium (about 4-6 feet tall)
    
- Speed: 30 feet
    

Darkvision. You have Darkvision with a range of 60 feet.

Devour Corpse. You can eat spoiled and rotten food as if it were fresh.

Additionally, if you spend 1 minute eating flesh from the corpse of a Small or larger creature that isn't a Construct, roll one of your [Hit Point Dice](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#hit%20point%20dice_xphb) without expending it (even if you have none remaining). You regain a number of [Hit Points](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#hit%20points_xphb) equal to the number rolled plus your Constitution modifier (minimum of 1 [Hit Point](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#hit%20points_xphb)). Once you regain [Hit Points](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#hit%20points_xphb) using this trait, you can't do so again until you finish a [Short](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#short%20rest_xphb) or [Long Rest](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#long%20rest_xphb).

Frozen Waste. You have [Resistance](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#resistance_xphb) to Cold damage.

Infused Drakkonite. When you damage a creature, you can ignore any [Resistances](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#resistance_xphb) it has, and it takes extra Necrotic damage equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#proficiency_xphb). Once you use this trait, you can't do so again until you finish a [Short](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#short%20rest_xphb) or [Long Rest](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#long%20rest_xphb).
