### Traits: BoET
- Ability Scores: Wisdom +1; Choose any +2; choose any other +1
    
- Creature Type: Humanoid
    
- Size: Medium
    
- Speed: 30 feet
    

Age. Your natural aging process slows and becomes similar to that of an elf or shadow fey.

Alignment. Umbral humans have often walked a troubling path to become what they are. This tends to push umbral humans toward neutral or chaotic alignments, but all variations exist.

Darkvision. You can see in [dim light](https://5e.tools/quickreference.html#bookref-quick,2,vision%20and%20light) within 60 feet as though it were [bright light](https://5e.tools/quickreference.html#bookref-quick,2,vision%20and%20light) and in darkness as if it were [dim light](https://5e.tools/quickreference.html#bookref-quick,2,vision%20and%20light). You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.

Dark Infusion. You have resistance to cold damage.

Fade Away. While standing perfectly still, you can use an action to become [invisible](https://5e.tools/conditionsdiseases.html#invisible_phb). You become visible again if you move or take an action. You can use this feature a number of times per day equal to your proficiency bonus.

Language. You can speak, read, and write Common and Umbral.

Cursed Infusion. You have resistance to necrotic damage.

Shadow Gift. You struck a bargain with a shadow fey to receive a gift, but it came at a terrible price. Choose one of the options below to determine the benefit and penalty you possess (or discuss other options with your GM):

- Increase one of your ability scores by 6 (up to 20). Reduce two different ability scores by 2.
- You no longer need to eat or breathe to survive and require only 4 hours for a long rest. Gain one level of [exhaustion](https://5e.tools/conditionsdiseases.html#exhaustion_phb). This level of [exhaustion](https://5e.tools/conditionsdiseases.html#exhaustion_phb) cannot be removed by any means magical or non-magical.
- Cut your current base movement speed in half. Gain a fly, swim, or climb speed equal to your newly halved base movement speed.
- You become proficient in one skill of your choice and always roll [[Ability Check]] made with that skill at advantage. Choose a second skill, and always roll [[Ability Check]] made with that skill at disadvantage.
- Each day at dawn, you gain [[Temporary Hit Point]] equal to your Constitution score. You are no longer able to spend hit dice to heal during short rests.
