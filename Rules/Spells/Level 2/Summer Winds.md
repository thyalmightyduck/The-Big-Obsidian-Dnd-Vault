_Level 2 Evocation_

**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** Self
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Instantaneous

You unleash a blast of light and wind in a 15-foot [[Cone]]. Your allies in the [[Cone]] regain 2d6 [[Hit Point]]. Undead within the [[Cone]] that are [[Hostile]] to you must make a Constitution saving throw, taking 2d6 Radiant damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The damage and healing increase by 2d6 for each spell slot level above 2.

**Classes:** [[Cleric]], [[Druid]], [[Paladin]], [[Ranger]]
**Subclasses:** [[Circle of Wicker Druid]], [[Harvest Domain Cleric]]
