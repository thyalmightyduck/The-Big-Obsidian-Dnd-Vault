_Illusion Level 2_

**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** Touch
**Components:** V, S, M (An Eyelash In Gum Arabic)
**Duration:** [[Concentration]], Up To 1 Hour

A creature you touch has the [[Invisible Condition]] condition until the spell ends. The spell ends early immediately after the target makes an [[Attack Rolls]], deals damage, or casts a spell.

Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot. You can target one additional creature for each spell slot level above 2.
