_Level 2 Abjuration_

**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** Touch
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** 1 hour

You touch a creature and end the [[Poisoned]] condition on it. For the duration, the target has [[Advantage]] on saving throws to avoid or end the [[Poisoned]] condition, and it has [[Resistance]] to Poison damage.

**Classes:** [[Apothecary]], [[Artificer]], [[Cleric]], [[Druid]], [[Paladin]], [[Ranger]], [[Tamer]]
**Subclasses:** [[College of Lore Bard]], [[Divine Soul Sorcerer]], [[Festus Domain Cleric]], [[Green Reaper Ranger]], [[skinshifter Sorcerer]], [[Strength Domain Cleric]]
**Backgrounds:** [[Golgari Agent]]
**Feats:** [[Adept of the White Robes]]
