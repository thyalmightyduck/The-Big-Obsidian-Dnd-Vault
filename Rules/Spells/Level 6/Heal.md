_Level 6 Abjuration_

**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** 60 feet
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Instantaneous

Choose a creature that you can see within range. Positive energy washes through the target, restoring 70 [[Hit Point]]. This spell also ends the [[Blinded]], [[Deafened]], and [[Poisoned]] conditions on the target.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot:** The healing increases by 10 for each spell slot level above 6.

**Classes:** [[Apothecary]], [[Cleric]], [[Druid]]
**Subclasses:** [[Divine Soul Sorcerer]]
