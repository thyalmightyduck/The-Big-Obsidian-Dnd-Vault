_Evocation Level 3_

**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** Self
**Components:** V, S, M (a bit of fur and a crystal rod)
**Duration:** Instantaneous

A stroke of lightning forming a 100-foot-long, 5-foot-wide [[Line]] blasts out from you in a direction you choose. Each creature in the [[Line]] makes a Dexterity saving throw, taking `dice: 8d6` 8d6 Lightning damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot:** The damage increases by 1d6 for each spell slot level above 3.
