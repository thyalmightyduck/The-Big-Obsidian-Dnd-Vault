_Illusion Level 3_

**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** 120 Feet
**Components:** S, M (A Pinch Of Confetti)
**Duration:** [[Concentration]]. Up To 1 Minute

You create a twisting pattern of colors in a 30-foot Cub within range. The pattern appears for a moment and vanishes. Each creature in the area who can see the pattern must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or have the [[Charmed]] condition for the duration. While [[Charmed]], the creature has the [[Incapacitated]] condition and a Speed of 0.

The spell ends for an affected creature if it takes any damage or if someone else uses an action to shake the creature out of its stupor.
