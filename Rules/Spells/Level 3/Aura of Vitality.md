# Player’s Handbook (2024):
_Evocation Level 3_

**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** Self 
**Components:** V
**Duration:** [[Concentration]], up to 1 Minute

An aura radiates from you in a 30-foot [[Emanation]] for the duration. When you create the aura and at the start of each of your turns while it persists, you can restore 2d6 [[Hit Point]] to one creature in it.

**Classes:** [[Paladin]]
**Subclasses:** [[Battle Smith Artificer]], [[College of Lore Bard]], [[Divine Soul Sorcerer]], [[Oath of the Crown Paladin]], [[Twilight Domain Cleric]]
**Optional/Variant Classes:** [[Cleric]], [[Druid]]
**Species:** [[Halfling (Mark Of Healing)]]
**Backgrounds:** [[Quandrix Student]]
