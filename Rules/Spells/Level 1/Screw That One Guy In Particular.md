_Evocation Level 1_

**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** Unlimited
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Instantaneous

A bolt of lightning streaks from the sky to strike one creature within range. The creature is randomly determined by the DM. The creature must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 3d10 lightning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

If this spell is cast again by any spellcaster within 1 minute, the same creature is struck

**Classes:** Sorcerer, Wizard
