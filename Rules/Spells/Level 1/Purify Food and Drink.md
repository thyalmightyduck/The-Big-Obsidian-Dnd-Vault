_Level 1 Transmutation_

**Casting Time:** Action or Ritual
**Range:** 10 feet
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Instantaneous

You remove poison and rot from nonmagical food and drink in a 5-foot-radius [[Sphere]] centered on a point within range.
