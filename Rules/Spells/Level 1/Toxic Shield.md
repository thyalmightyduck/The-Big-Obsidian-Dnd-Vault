_Conjuration Level 1_

**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** Self
**Components:** S, M (a drop of dwarven blood)
**Duration:** [[Concentration]], up to 1 minute

You gain resistance to poison damage and advantage on saves against being [[poisoned]] for the duration. In addition, whenever a creature within 5 feet of you hits you with a melee attack, the shield sprays them with poison, dealing `dice: 1d8` 1d8 poison damage to the attacker.

**At Higher Levels:** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the poison damage increases by 1d8 for every 2 slot levels above 1st.

**Classes:** [[Apothecary]], [[Bard]], [[Paladin]], [[Warlock]]
**Subclasses:** [[Hand of Dread Pugilist]]
