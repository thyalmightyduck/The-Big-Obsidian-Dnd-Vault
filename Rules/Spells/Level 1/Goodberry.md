_Level 1 Conjuration_

**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** Touch
**Components:** V, S, M (a sprig of mistletoe)
**Duration:** 24 hours

Ten berries appear in your hand and are infused with magic for the duration. A creature can take a [[Bonus Action]] to eat one berry. Eating a berry restores 1 [[Hit Points]], and the berry provides enough nourishment to sustain a creature for one day.

Uneaten berries disappear when the spell ends.

**Classes:** [[Druid]], [[Ranger]]
**Subclasses:** [[College of Lore Bard]], [[Festus Domain Cleric]], [[Harvest Domain Cleric]], [[Trail Warden Ranger]]
**Species:** [[Halfling (Mark of Hospitality)]]
**Feats:** [[Divinely Favored]], [[Magic Initiate]], [[Magic Initiate]], [[Mark of Hospitality]], [[Rune Shaper]], [[Strixhaven Initiate]]
