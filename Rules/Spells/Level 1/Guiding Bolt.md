_Evocation Level 1_

**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** 120 Feet
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** 1 Round

You hurl a bolt of light toward a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, it takes 4d6 Radiant damage, and the next [[Attack Rolls]] made against it before the end of your next turn has [[Advantage]].

Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot. The damage increases by 1d6 for each spell slot level above 1.
