_Necromancy Level 1_

**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** Touch
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Instantaneous

A creature you touch makes a Constitution saving throw, taking `dice: 2d10` 2d10 Necrotic damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot:** The damage increases by 1d10 for each spell slot level above 1.
