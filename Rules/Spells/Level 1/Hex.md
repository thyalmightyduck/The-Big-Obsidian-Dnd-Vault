_Enchantment Level 1_

**Casting Time:** [[Bonus Action]]
**Range:** 90 Feet
**Components:** V, S, M (the petrified eye of a newt)
**Duration:** [[Concentration]], Up To 1 hour

You place a curse on a creature that you can see within range. Until the spell ends, you deal an extra 1d6 Necrotic damage to the target whenever you hit it with an [[Attack Rolls]]. Also, choose one ability when you cast the spell. The target has Disadvantage on [[Ability Check]] made with the chosen ability.

If the target drops to 0 [[Hit Point]] before this spell ends, you can take a [[Bonus Action]] on a later turn to curse a new creature.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot:** Your Concentration can last longer with a spell slot of level 2 (up to 4 hours), 3-4 (up to 8 hours), or 5+ (24 hours).
