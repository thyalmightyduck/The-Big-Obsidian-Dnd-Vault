_Abjuration Level 1_

**Casting Time:** [[Bonus Action]]
**Range:** Self
**Components:** V, S, M (A Shard Of Blue Glass)
**Duration:** 1 Hour

Protective magical frost surrounds you. You gain 5 [[Temporary Hit Points]]. If a creature hits you with a melee [[Attack Rolls]] before the spell ends, the creature takes 5 Cold damage. The spell ends early if you have no [[Temporary Hit Points]].

Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot. The [[Temporary Hit Points]] and the Cold damage both increase by 5 for each spell slot level above 1.
