_Level 1 Transmutation_

**Casting Time:** Bonus action
**Range:** Self
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** 1 minute

Until the spell ends, your attacks with weapons deal an extra 1d4 Radiant damage on a hit.

**Classes:** [[Paladin]]
**Subclasses:** [[Leader Tamer]], [[Oath of Castigation Paladin]], [[War Domain Cleric]], [[Strength Domain Cleric]]
