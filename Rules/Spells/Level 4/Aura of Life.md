_Level 4 Abjuration_

**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** Self
**Components:** V
**Duration:** [[Concentration]], up to 10 minutes

An aura radiates from you in a 30-foot [[Emanation]] for the duration. While in the aura, you and your allies have [[Resistance]] to Necrotic damage, and your [[Hit Points]] maximums can't be reduced. If an ally with 0 [[Hit Points]] starts its turn in the aura, that ally regains 1 [[Hit Points]].

**Classes:** [[Cleric]], [[Paladin]]
**Subclasses:** [[Circle of Wildfire Druid]], [[Divine Soul Sorcerer]], [[Life Domain Cleric]], [[The Undying Warlock]], [[Twilight Domain Cleric]], [[Solidarity Domain Cleric]]
**Species:** [[Halfling (Mark of Healing)]], [[Human (Mark of Handling)]]
**Backgrounds:** [[Selesnya Initiate]]
**Feats:** [[Mark of Handling]], [[Mark of Healing]]
