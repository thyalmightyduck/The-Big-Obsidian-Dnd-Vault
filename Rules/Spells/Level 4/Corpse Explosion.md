_Necromancy Level 4_

**Casting Time:** Reaction, which you take when a creature you can see dies within 120 feet of you.
**Range:** 120 Feet
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Instantaneous

You cause the triggering creature's body to explode in a violent shower of gore and infected flesh. Creatures within 10 feet of a point centered on the triggering creature's body take `dice:4d8` 4d8 necrotic damage and `dice:4d8` 4d8 acid damage, or half as much damage on a successful Dexterity saving throw.

**At Higher Levels:** If you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 acid damage for each slot level above 4th.

**Classes:** [[Apothecary]], [[Warlock]], [[Wizard]]
**Subclasses:** [[Hand of Dread Pugilist]], [[Occultist Guild Monster Hunter]], [[Reanimator Apothecary]], [[Sanguine Thief Rogue]], [[Spellslinger Gunslinger]], [[Way of the Arcane Hand Monk]]
