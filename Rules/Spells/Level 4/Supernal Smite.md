_Level 4 Evocation_

**Casting Time:** Bonus action, which you take immediately after hitting a creature with a Melee weapon or an Unarmed Strike
**Range:** Self
**Components:** V
**Duration:** Instantaneous

Your strike cause ambient magic surrounded a creature to collapse and detonate. The target hit by the strike takes an extra 4d6 Force damage from the attack. If the creature has [[Concentration]] on a spell, that [[Concentration]] is broken.
In addition, each ongoing spell of level 3 or lower on the target ends. For each ongoing spell of level 4 or higher on the target, make an ability check using your spellcasting ability (DC 10 plus that spell's level). On a successful check, the spell ends.

**Classes:** [[Paladin]]
**Subclasses:** [[Oath of Slaughter Paladin]]
