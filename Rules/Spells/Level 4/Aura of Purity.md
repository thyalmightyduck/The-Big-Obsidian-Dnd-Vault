# Player’s Handbook (2024):
_Abjuration Level 4_

**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** Self
**Components:** V
**Duration:** [[Concentration]], up to 10 minutes

An aura radiates from you in a 30-foot [[Emanation]] for the duration. While in the aura, you and your allies have [[Resistance]] to Poison damage and [[Advantage]] on saving throws to avoid or end effects that include the [[Blinded]], [[Charmed]], [[Deafened]], [[Frightened]], [[Paralyzed]], [[Poisoned]], or [[Stunned]] condition.

**Classes:** [[Cleric]], [[Paladin]]
**Subclasses:** [[Battle Smith Artificer]], [[Divine Soul Sorcerer]], [[Oath of Watchers Paladin]], [[Peace Domain Cleric]], [[Purification Domain Cleric]]
**Species:** [[Halfling (Mark of Healing)]], [[Halfling (Mark of Hospitality)]]
**Feats:** [[Mark of Healing]], [[Mark of Hospitality]]
