# Player’s Handbook (2014)
_Abjuration Cantrip_

**Casting Time:**  Action
**Range:** Touch
**Components:** V, S, M (a miniature cloak)
**Duration:** [[Concentration]], up to 1 Minute

You touch one willing creature. Once before the spell ends, the target can roll a d4 and add the number rolled to one [[saving throw]] of its choice. It can roll the die before or after making the [[saving throw]]. The spell then ends.

**Subclasses:** [[Circle of the Land Druid]], [[College of Lore Bard]], [[College of the Moon Bard]], [[Dark Petitioner Rogue]], [[Divine Soul Sorcerer]], [[Nature Domain Cleric]], [[Strength Domain Cleric]]
**Species:** [[Elf]], [[Elf (Kaladesh Vahadar)]], [[Khoravar]], [[Merfolk (Ixalan Green)]], [[Merfolk (Zendikar Emeria Creed)]]
**Feats:** [[Artificer Initiate]], [[Blessed Warrior]], [[Divinely Favored]], [[Druidic Warrior]], [[Light Bringer]], [[Magic Initiate]], [[Magic Initiate]], [[Wood Elf Magic]]
