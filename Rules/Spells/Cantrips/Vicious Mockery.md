_Enchantment Cantrip_

**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** 60 Feet
**Components:** V
**Duration:** Instantaneous

You unleash a string of insults laced with subtle enchantments at one creature you can see or hear within range. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or take 1d6 Psychic damage and have Disadvantage on the next [[Attack Rolls]] it makes before the end of its next turn.

**Cantrip Upgrade:** The damage increases by 1d6 when you reach levels 5 (2d6), 11 (3d6), and 17 (4d6).

**Subclasses:** [[Architect of Ruin Illrigger]], [[College of Fools Bard]], [[Great Fool Patron Warlock]]
