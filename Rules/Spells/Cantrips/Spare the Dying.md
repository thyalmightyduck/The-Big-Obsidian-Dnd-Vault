# 2024
_Necromancy Cantrip_

**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** 15 feet
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Instantaneous


Choose a creature within range that has 0 [[Hit Point]] and isn't dead. The creature becomes Stable.

Cantrip Upgrade. The range doubles when you reach levels 5 (30 feet), 11 (60 feet), and 17 (120 feet).

# 2014

_Necromancy Cantrip_

**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** Self
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Instantaneous
