_Level 5 Transmutation_

**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** Touch
**Components:** V, S, M (a four-leaf clover and a shard of broken mirror)
**Duration:** [[Concentration]], up to 1 hour

You touch a Tiny, nonmagical object, infusing it with a portion of your own luck for the duration. While another creature carries the object, that creature has [[Advantage]] on [[D20 Test]]s, and you have [[Disadvantage]] on [[D20 Test]]s.

**Classes:** [[Bard]], [[Paladin]], [[Ranger]], [[Warlock]]
**Subclasses:** [[Circle of Wicker Druid]], [[Hand of Dread Pugilist]]
