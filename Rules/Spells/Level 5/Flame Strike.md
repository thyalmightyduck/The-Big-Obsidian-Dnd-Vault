_Evocation Level 5_

**Casting Time:** Action
**Range:** 60 Feet
**Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of sulfur)
**Duration:** Instantaneous

A vertical column of brilliant fire roars down from above. Each creature in a 10-foot-radius, 40-foot-high [[Cylinder]]  centered on a point within range makes a Dexterity saving throw, taking `dice:5d6` 5d6 Fire damage and `dice:5d6` 5d6 Radiant damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot:** The Fire damage and the Radiant damage increase by 1d6 for each spell slot level above 5.
