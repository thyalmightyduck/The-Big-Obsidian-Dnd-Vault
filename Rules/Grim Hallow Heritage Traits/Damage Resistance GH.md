Exposure to the worst effects of a specific energy has given you a tolerance to its effects. You have [[Rules/Core Rules/Resistance]] to one of the following damage types of your choice: Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, Poison, or Thunder.

Damage Immunity. If you take this trait twice, as a [[Reaction]] to taking damage of the type you chose for [[Damage]] [[Rules/Core Rules/Resistance]], you gain [[Immunity]] to that damage type until the end of your next turn. You regain the use this feature when you finish a [[Short Rest]].
