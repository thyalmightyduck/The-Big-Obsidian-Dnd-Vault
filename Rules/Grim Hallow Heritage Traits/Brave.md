The horrors you've lived through have hardened you. You have [Advantage](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#advantage_xphb) on saving throws to avoid being [Frightened](https://5e.tools/conditionsdiseases.html#frightened_xphb).

Infectious Bravery. If you take this trait twice, you can use your [Reaction](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#reaction_xphb) to bolster the spirits of your allies, granting one ally who can see or hear you [Advantage](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#advantage_xphb) on a saving throw against being [Frightened](https://5e.tools/conditionsdiseases.html#frightened_xphb). You can use this feature a number of times equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#proficiency_xphb), regaining all expended uses when you finish a [Long Rest](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#long%20rest_xphb).
