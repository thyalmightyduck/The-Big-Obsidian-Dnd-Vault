The dangers of Etharis have given you a focus that allows you to shrug off debilitating magical effects. You automatically succeed on saving throws against magical effects that would give you the [Incapacitated](https://5e.tools/conditionsdiseases.html#incapacitated_xphb), [Stunned](https://5e.tools/conditionsdiseases.html#stunned_xphb), or [Unconscious](https://5e.tools/conditionsdiseases.html#unconscious_xphb) conditions. This does not include effects that leave you [Unconscious](https://5e.tools/conditionsdiseases.html#unconscious_xphb) because you are reduced to 0 [Hit Points](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#hit%20points_xphb).

Reawakened. If you take this trait twice, you also have [Advantage](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#advantage_xphb) on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws.
