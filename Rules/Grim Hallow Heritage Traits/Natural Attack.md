The gift of natural weaponry means you are never unarmed, as your foes learn to their peril. Your [Unarmed Strikes](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#unarmed%20strike_xphb) deal damage equal to 1d6 + your Strength or Dexterity modifier. The type of damage dealt by your [Unarmed Strikes](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#unarmed%20strike_xphb) can be Bludgeoning, Piercing, or Slashing, based on the type of natural weaponry you possess (claws, horns, a tail, and so forth).

Swift Strike. If you take this trait twice, you can use [Unarmed Strike](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#unarmed%20strike_xphb) as a [Bonus Action](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#bonus%20action_xphb). You can use this feature a number of times equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#proficiency_xphb), regaining all expended uses when you finish a [Long Rest](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#long%20rest_xphb).
