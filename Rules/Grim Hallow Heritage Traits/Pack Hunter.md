Fighting in the thick of battle lets you aid your allies when it counts. When an ally within 10 feet of you is about to make an [[Attack Rolls]] or a saving throw, you can use a [Reaction](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#reaction_xphb) to grant that ally [Advantage](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#advantage_xphb) on the attack or save. You can use this feature a number of times equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#proficiency_xphb), regaining all expended uses when you finish a [Long Rest](https://5e.tools/variantrules.html#long%20rest_xphb).

Pack Leader. If you take this trait twice, Pack Hunter can be triggered by any ally within 30 feet of you. Additionally, if the [[Attack Rolls]] misses or the saving throw fails, you don't lose that usage of Pack Hunter.
