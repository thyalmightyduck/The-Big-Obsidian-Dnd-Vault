_100 GP_

As a [[Bonus Action]], you can use a [[vial]] of Basic Poison to coat one weapon or up to three pieces of ammunition. A creature that takes Piercing or Slashing damage from the [[poisoned]] weapon or ammunition takes an extra `dice: 1d4` Poison damage. Once applied, the poison retains potency for 1 minute or until its damage is dealt, whichever comes first.
